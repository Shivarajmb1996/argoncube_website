The ArgonCube Team

During March 21st and 22nd 2019, a fraction of all members of the ArgonCube team met in Bern (image above).

The full team consists of more than 100 physicists from 30 institutes:
Argonne National Laboratory (ANL), University of Texas at Arlington (UTA), University of Bern, Brookhaven National Laboratory (BNL), University of California - Berkeley, University of California - Davis, University of California - Irvine, University of California - Santa Barbara, California Institute of Technology (Caltech), University of Cambridge, Colorado State University (CSU), Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory (FNAL), University of Houston, Joint Institute for Nuclear Research (JINR), Lancaster University, Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (LBNL), University of Manchester, Michigan State University (MSU), University of Minnesota Duluth, University of Pennsylvania, University of Rochester, Rutgers University, University of Sheffield, SLAC National Accelerator Laboratory, Tufts University, University of Warwick, Wichita State University, William and Marry University, Yale University, York University.
