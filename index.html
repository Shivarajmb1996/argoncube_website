ArgonCube is a novel design for building advanced Liquid Argon Time Projection Chambers (TPCs) .
This design is based on detector modularisation, enabling the scalability to large active detector masses. ArgonCube developed new technologies for a more robust and reliable TPC operation, including a pixelated charge readout, light detectors with a precise time resolution, and a new approach to produce and shape electric fields using a highly resistive sheet. The ArgonCube design will be applied in DUNE , a next-generation neutrino experiment.
